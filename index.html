About Development Discourses

Development Discourses is a curated open-access library by ImpactMojo — a platform for free development economics and policy education in South Asia.

This collection brings together research papers, books, and grey literature across the themes that matter to development practitioners: from monitoring & evaluation to climate resilience, from gender justice to public health, from data governance to livelihoods.

We prioritize open-access resources — free to read, cite, and share. Each resource is tagged with its access status: Open Access, Free to Read, or Check Access. We believe knowledge should not sit behind paywalls, especially knowledge about building a more equitable world.
